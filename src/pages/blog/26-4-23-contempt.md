I hate the word vulnerable.

People think that vulgar word lies left of humility?

Any Konosuba enjoyer would know:

Relationships without conflict

are fake.

Without contempt

you cannot distinguish yourself

from the dirt beneath your feet.

Only by rejecting the world as it is

can your dreams ever be more than

wisps in the air.

social media is stupid.

Most media leaves

as much impression on you

as pure white noise.

The world is not good.

How could it be, when you've yet to change it?
